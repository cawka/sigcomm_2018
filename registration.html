A Special Note on Author Registration Policy

Each accepted paper/poster must be presented by one of its author(s) at the SIGCOMM main conference or workshop. The author must register for the corresponding event according to his/her membership status: e.g., for a student author presenting a paper/poster, he/she only needs to register under the student category. We do not require a full-rate registration for each paper.

Extras

All conference registrations (Member / Non-member / Student) include breakfasts, coffee breaks, and lunches on Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday, a welcome reception, a banquet dinner and a conference kit. In addition to these items, a student registered for the conference will also receive a ticket to the student dinner.

Workshop / Tutorial registration includes breakfast, coffee breaks, lunch on the day of the registered Workshop / Tutorial. (Welcome Reception and banquet dinner are not included.)
